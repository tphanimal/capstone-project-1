Hello everyone, thanks for taking the time getting to know me and reading my hobbies page! It's always wonderful to get to know what excites us.

Wandering the trails around Austin, Texas, with my golden retriever, Zoe, is like therapy for the soul. Her boundless energy and infectious enthusiasm make every hike an adventure. We've explored the hidden gems of the Texas Hill Country, from the lush greenery of Barton Creek Greenbelt to the rugged beauty of Balcones Canyonlands. There's something magical about the early morning light filtering through the trees or stumbling upon a quiet spot by the water – those moments stay with you.

Beyond our beloved Austin trails, I'm a bit of a travel enthusiast. There's nothing like the thrill of discovering new places, immersing myself in different cultures, and collecting memories from around the globe. Whether it's strolling through the vibrant streets of European cities or getting lost in the tranquility of Southeast Asian landscapes, each journey adds a new layer to my perspective.

Photography has become my way of freezing these moments in time. It's not about perfect shots but capturing the essence of a place – the warmth of a sunlit alleyway, the genuine smiles of locals, or the awe-inspiring beauty of a breathtaking vista. My camera is my companion in telling the stories of the places I've been fortunate enough to explore.

These hobbies aren't just a way to unwind; they're an integral part of who I am. They bring balance to my life, infusing it with inspiration and a sense of fulfillment. Whether it's the simplicity of a local hike, the excitement of a new adventure abroad, or the joy of preserving memories through photographs, these pursuits shape the human side of my journey.
